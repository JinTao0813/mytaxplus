## Overview

This document defines progressive tax rate categories based on chargeable income.

## Progressive Tax Explanation

Malaysia uses a **progressive tax system**, meaning:

- Each portion of income is taxed at a different rate
- Higher income segments are taxed at higher rates

### Example Structure

For an income in Category D (RM 40,000):

- First RM 5,000 → 0%
- Next RM 15,000 → 1%
- Next RM 15,000 → 3%
- Remaining RM 5,000 → 6%

---

## Notes

- Tax values shown are **cumulative base + marginal tax**
- "Next" refers to the portion within that bracket only
- Always calculate tax incrementally across brackets
