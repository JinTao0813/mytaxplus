# Malaysia Individual Tax Relief Rules (YA 2025)

## Overview

This document defines the available individual tax relief types and their maximum claimable amounts.

---

## 1. Individual and Dependent Relatives

- **Amount:** RM 9,000

---

## 2. Parents and Grandparents (Medical & Care)

- Medical treatment, dental treatment, special needs, and carer expenses  
  _(Medical condition must be certified by a medical practitioner)_
- Complete medical examination _(limited to RM 1,000)_
- **Total Relief Limit:** RM 8,000 (Restricted)

---

## 3. Basic Supporting Equipment (Disabled)

- For disabled self, spouse, child, or parent
- **Amount:** RM 6,000 (Restricted)

---

## 4. Disabled Individual

- **Amount:** RM 7,000

---

## 5. Education Fees (Self)

- Non-masters/PhD: Law, accounting, Islamic financing, technical, vocational, industrial, scientific, or technology courses
- Masters/PhD: Any course
- Upskilling/self-enhancement _(limited to RM 2,000)_
- **Total Relief Limit:** RM 7,000 (Restricted)

---

## 6. Medical Expenses (Serious Conditions)

- Serious diseases (self, spouse, child)
- Fertility treatment (self or spouse)
- Vaccination _(limited to RM 1,000)_
- Dental examination and treatment _(limited to RM 1,000)_
- **Total Relief Limit:** RM 10,000 (Restricted)

---

## 7. Health Screening & Tests

- Complete medical examination
- COVID-19 detection test (including self-test kits)
- Mental health consultation
- Self-health monitoring equipment
- Disease detection tests
- **Total Relief Limit:** RM 1,000 (Restricted)

---

## 8. Child Development (≤ 18 years old)

- Intellectual disability assessment
- Early intervention / rehabilitation programs
- **Total Relief Limit:** RM 6,000 (Restricted)

---

## 9. Lifestyle (General)

- Books, journals, magazines (non-prohibited)
- Personal computer, smartphone, tablet _(non-business use)_
- Internet subscription (under own name)
- Skill improvement courses
- **Total Relief Limit:** RM 2,500 (Restricted)

---

## 10. Lifestyle (Sports)

- Sports equipment
- Rental/entry fees for sports facilities
- Competition registration fees (approved organizers)
- Gym membership / training
- **Total Relief Limit:** RM 1,000 (Restricted)

---

## 11. Breastfeeding Equipment

- For child aged ≤ 2 years
- Claimable once every 2 years
- **Amount:** RM 1,000 (Restricted)

---

## 12. Child Care Fees

- Registered childcare centre / kindergarten
- Child aged ≤ 6 years
- **Amount:** RM 3,000 (Restricted)

---

## 13. SSPN (Education Savings)

- Net deposit = Total deposit − Total withdrawal (same year)
- **Amount:** RM 8,000 (Restricted)

---

## 14. Spouse / Alimony

- Husband / wife / alimony to former wife
- **Amount:** RM 4,000 (Restricted)

---

## 15. Disabled Spouse

- **Amount:** RM 6,000

---

## 16. Children Relief

### 16a. Child (< 18 years old)

- **Amount:** RM 2,000 per child

### 16b. Child (≥ 18 years old, unmarried, studying full-time)

- Pre-university (A-Level, matriculation, etc.)
  - RM 2,000 per child
- Diploma or higher (Malaysia) OR Degree or higher (Overseas, approved institutions)
  - RM 8,000 per child

### 16c. Disabled Child

- Base relief: RM 8,000
- Additional (≥ 18 years old, studying diploma+):
  - Additional RM 8,000

---

## 17. Life Insurance & EPF

- EPF contributions _(limit RM 4,000)_
- Life insurance / takaful _(limit RM 3,000)_
- **Total Relief Limit:** RM 7,000 (Restricted)

---

## 18. Deferred Annuity & PRS

- **Amount:** RM 3,000 (Restricted)

---

## 19. Education & Medical Insurance

- **Amount:** RM 4,000 (Restricted)

---

## 20. SOCSO Contribution

- **Amount:** RM 350 (Restricted)

---

## 21. Green Expenses

- EV charging facilities
- Domestic food waste composting machine _(non-business use)_
- **Amount:** RM 2,500 (Restricted)

---

## 22. Housing Loan Interest (First Home)

_(SPA between 1 Jan 2025 – 31 Dec 2027)_

- Property ≤ RM 500,000 → **RM 7,000**
- Property RM 500,001 – RM 750,000 → **RM 5,000**

---

## Notes

- "Restricted" means capped at the specified maximum.
- All claims must be supported with valid documentation.
- Eligibility conditions (e.g., age, relationship, certification) must be met.
